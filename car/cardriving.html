Maunal Car Driving Method in English


  • First of all, sit in the car and adjust your seat and also adjust the side mirrors.

  • After that, before starting the vehicle, check that the gear is in neutral and lower the handbrake.

  • Now start the vehicle and engage the first gear by fully depressing the clutch and slowly release the clutch.
    clutch_break_race/accelerator
    And when the clutch is fully released, start racing if needed.
    Similarly, if you want to engage the second gear, press the clutch again and slowly release the clutch by engaging shift 2 and you can engage all the gears in the same way.

  • While driving, keep moving the steering wheel slightly so that the car travels straight. Do not engage the clutch and race at the same time and use the clutch to brake.

  • If you want to slow down the speed, leave the race and depress the clutch, go to the utron and apply the brake and fully release the steering wheel and straighten the steering wheel by releasing the clutch and the race a little.

  • Whenever you come close to the uturn, reduce the speed with the help of clutch and brake and stop the race while taking the uturn and while reducing the gear, go to the uturn and turn the steering wheel fully and when the car turns completely, straighten the steering immediately.

  • Or apply the brakes near the utron and turn the steering wheel full circle with the vehicle moving slowly and straighten it immediately.

    • * Reverse Gair *

  • To engage the reverse gear, engage the reverse shift and turn the steering wheel in the direction in which the vehicle is to be turned while keeping the foot on the clutch and brake.
