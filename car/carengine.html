Car Engine ( English )


  • First open the lid lock from inside and then open from outside and check the battery nuts they should be tight and the battery level should be according to the scale above.

  • The function of the battery is only to start the vehicle, after that the generator does all the work and the generator itself charges the battery.

  • The engine has a radiator in a steel body whose job is to keep the engine temperature low. The CH point in the vehicle meter indicates the heat of the radiator. If a green light is visible here, it means that the radiator is hot. The temperature is normal, but if the red light is visible, it means that the radiator has become hot enough. In such a situation, stop the car and stand at a short distance with the lid open, and pour cold water on the radiator so that its temperature is reduced. Be less but add water carefully so that it does not go into another engine, then be patient, when it cools down, open the lid of this radiator and fill it with water, but remember not to open the lid in hot temperatures because It has fire that circulates and can come straight out and hit you in the mouth.

  • Fill the radiator with water once a day

  • Check the brake and brake fluid regularly. The maximum and minimum ranges are written on the bottles.

  • Change the oil after driving every 3000 km. By the way, each company has its own instructions. You can estimate the oil level by taking out a thin iron wire in a pipe with an oil tank.

  • Also check the air filters regularly if this does not work the engine will overheat and if the radiator fan is turned off the engine and radiator will overheat.

  • Inside the engine, three gasoline pipelines are connected to the main pipe with a small motor. The longer the gasoline pipeline, the more power the vehicle will have. For example, there is one in a motorcycle. If you start the car, there will be current in it, which means that the motor is working properly

  • If you ever have a problem with a tire or get a puncture, you can take a dummy tire from the back and use the tools to change the tire. They can remove the tire and replace it and replace it as soon as they fix the tire. There should also be a tire-releasing T-tool inside the car and a car lifter.
