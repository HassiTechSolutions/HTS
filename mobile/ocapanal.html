OCA Panal Dissembly.( English )

    Old Method.

  • Ninety percent customer approval is taken in case of panel damage.
  • Place the mobile phone upside down on the LCD sprayer machine with a temperature of 98 degrees Celsius.
  • It is easy to change the LCD panel but it is difficult to change the glass. To change the glass of the LCD panel, the LCD panel is first heated and removed with the help of the LCD sprayer machine, and then the panel is reattached. The glass is carefully removed by heating with the help of a machine.
  • After removing the glass, clean it from the LCD screen, you can use alcohol for this. After cleaning, apply UV glue on the screen to install a new glass, and then carefully place the glass on the LCD with the help of the UV gun. Coordinate the UV glow from and after that check by fitting the panel again in the mobile.
  • If there is too much glue on the screen from the LCD, give it a little temperature on the LCD sprayer and rub the glue off with your finger, but do not heat it too much or the LCD will break.

    New Method.

  • OCA is a new way to change the paper glass. To change the glass of some mobile phones, the LED panel is removed and a break between the glass and the screen is rubbed with a steel wire and then cleaned. OCA paper is placed on top of it and after some time this paper is removed and the panel is placed on it and the panel is fitted in the same way.
  • Generally, before installing the glass on the LCD, confirm whether the LCD is working or not by attaching the LCD to the mobile and then install the glass.
  • After removing the OCA paper and applying the glass, place it in the OCA machine to press it well, so adjust the OCA machine.
  • If bubbles appear in the panel after installation, the bubbles can be removed with a bubble removal machine.
