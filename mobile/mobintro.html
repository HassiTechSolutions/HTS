Introduction of Mobile Phone ( English )

The first mobile phone was invented in America by a man named Martin Cooper together with the Motorola team. The weight was about two kilograms. It worked under 1G technology. Later, 2G technology came in 1983 and 3G technology came in the market in 1992. AT-T Company of America introduced mobile phones for the first time on commercial basis in 1947. Mobile phone calls were connected by an operator and the person holding the phone had to press a special button to listen to the phone and make a call. While the mobile phone service was very expensive in this regard, when the mobile phone was invented, its battery time was only up to thirty minutes and you could only talk for thirty minutes while fully charging it. It took ten hours to do it, it worked without a SIM and it was called AMPS which means Advanced Mobile Phone System. Later, when SIM was invented, it started to be called GSMS i.e. Global System of Mobile and it was working under 2G technology. Its frequency is called megahertz, currently different frequencies are working all over the world like

900 MHz, 1800 MHz, 850 MHz, 1900 MHz, 2300 MHz

900 and 1800 MHz frequencies are working in Pakistan and in addition 3G and 4G have also started working. is called by different names in the world like mobile phone, cell phone, telephone, and cell phone etc. Mobile phone towers have a range of 26 kilometers within the area you can receive a signal from this tower, the signal weakens further away. 3G is a very fast network, the speed of this network depends on the antenna. In any mobile, the communication function is with the antenna, because its function is to receive and send the signal. It is also possible to print from the mobile phone. For this to work mobile and printer must have Wi-Fi and Bluetooth and its driver and application must be installed on the mobile.

900 MHz, 1800MHz, 850MHz, 1900MHz, 2300MHz
