Integrated Circuits ( IC ) in English

  • Leg IC are used in old Mobile Technology , and its legs are surrounded on its sides.
  • Bubble IC are used in new Mobile Technology , and its legs are placed under it which are attached with its nearby Capacitors .

Charging IC

Its job is to control the charging in the mobile - it is near the power IC - it is also a BGAIC - there is a large coded resistor near it,
its breakdown causes various charging problems.
It seems that when a mobile phone has a charging problem, first plug in another charger and then check the parts near the charging jack on the board or check the points of the charging jack and then the charging jack. Change If still not solved then charging IC is the problem Charging IC is replaced Some new mobiles have charging IC built inside the CPUIC then for this The entire CPU has to be replaced and the thing is that the CPUIC does not change

CPU IC

Its job is to command the mobile. It is large in size and its four corners are equal. And its Corners are attatched with golden wire which seems like pins but its actually not are pins.On disorder of this IC, the mobile dies and it is the only IC that does not change

RAM IC

It is also called temporary memory, its work starts when the mobile is turned on and ends when it is turned off, and the call record is also stored in it. Mobile does not turn on, when this IC get get damaged or out of order.

ROM IC

It is also called future memory, it contains the complete program of the mobile, it is installed near the CPU and RAM, due to its damage, the mobile does not turn on, when this IC get damaged or out of order.

PA IC

Due to this defect, the mobile signal does not come and if there is a short circuit, the battery is exhausted during the call. The IC's function is to block the incoming signal, it is neither BGA nor PGA, but it has bad points.

Antinna Switch

Its function is to switch between the incoming call and the frequency. No signal will come on mobile , when this will be damage or disordered.

RF IC

This is the main IC of the signal section, its job is to control all incoming calls, it is close to the PAIC and antenna thought, it is small in size, and the filter and oscillator are placed close to it. It happens that the signal does not come due to its deterioration

Rx and Tx Filters

Its function is to filter the frequency.They are generally of two types.Both function is to generate the frequency. Even when the signal is full, there is a problem with incoming and outgoing calls

Audio IC

Its job is to control the ringer speaker and mic. It is placed in the middle of most mobiles. It is a BGAIC and is placed near the power IC. Its damage causes audio problems in mobiles.

Power IC

Its job is to give power to the whole mobile. It is a BGAIC. It is also placed in the middle of the mobile. Near it is a large size polar capacitor and a long piece of glass or black color. It is connected and the mobile does not turn on even if it happens.
