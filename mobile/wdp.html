Water Damage Phones ( English )
  • Clean the water damaged phone board first with CTC. If the board is badly damaged, it does not need to be wash with CTC, it cannot be repaired.
  • If ever a mobile falls into water or anything, do not try to turn it on and do not heat it in the sun, but first clean the board with CTC and dry it thoroughly. And then turn it on

Water Damage Phones ( Urdu )
