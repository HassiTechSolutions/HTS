Charging Jack ( English )
  • There are different types of charging jacks.If you want to change the charging jack, first identify the charging jack.
  • First remove the damaged jack with the help of heat gun and clean its base first to install new jack, then you can install new jack by applying paste with soldering iron and using heat gun again.
  • Often there are five points of the charging jack, of which the first and last points are positive and negative, charging is done from these two points, and the middle three points are necessary for connecting to the computer.
  • If the jack is not recognized and there is a fear of installing the jack upside down, check the body of the mobile to see how the jack will be straight and if you want to identify the positive and negative points, you can use a multimeter.
  • The iPhone mobile does not have a charging jack, but the entire charging unit has to be installed from the market. If the power button of the iPhone is damaged, then the entire strap has to be changed.
  • If the charger jack of a mobile works but it is a little tight while working or has any problem, then in this case, clean the jack because it is often tight due to carbon on the jack. Be it charging jack or handsfree jack, clean it with CTC.
  • Often the Type-C jack is changed and more often than not, the entire Type-C charging unit of the model has to be replaced.
  • The charging jack of Android mobile is also called eighty-six hundred pin because this pin was first installed by Nokia in its eighty-six hundred model, hence it was named eighty-six hundred pin.

Charging Jack ( Urdu )
