Repairing Tools Usage ( English )

Soldering Iron.

Kavya is used for soldering or desoldering. It is heated by electricity. It is widely used in the field of electronics. Power button sim jacket and charging jack etc will always solder them with Kavia. Kavia is also used in jumpering.

  • Always use Kavya after it is properly warmed.
  • Always keep the Kavya in the stand after the work is done.
  • Never heat the plastic with the Kavya tip or rub it hard with a hard object.
  • If the Kavya tip turns black, clean it with a paste and a form moistened with water.

Hot Air Gun .

It is widely used in mobile phones and electronics atomizers - the same machine is used to remove and install parts that are SMDs on PCBs, and it emits hot air. Air and heat can be adjusted as needed - If a part is dry soldered on the PCB, it is repaired with the help of a hot air gun.

  • Always turn it on when needed and turn it off immediately after work.
  • On top of the mobile board, because the parts are too many, keep the nozzle size brake because the board parts are small.
  • Do not heat the IC too much otherwise the points below it will touch each other and there is a risk of destroying the mobile.

Eye Glass.

This tool is used to see the small parts on the mobile board.

Soldering Wire.

It is a fine silver color wire that can be used to solder these parts on the board. There are many types of wire available in the market but always use good quality wire.

Jumper Wire.

It is a thin wire with a green red or blue or gold colored chrome wire on it. It should be used in Fine Quality.

Soldering Paste.

It is a chemical and is applied on the board before soldering and desoldering. Applying this paste makes the parts come off easily and holds the solder quickly when we apply the soldering wire to the cable. So little smoke comes out of it because soldering wire also has little amount of paste before installing any new part it should be soldered with paste and silvered with soldering wire one is soldering paste and other is IC paste. You should always buy soldering paste.

Multimeter .

During electronics and mobile phone repair, multimeter is used to check the parts and board and to find out the value or to check the circuit and to check the DC voltage of the battery. can be checked but ICs are not checked with this - multimeter must be properly adjusted during operation.

Screw Drivers.

There are many types of screwdrivers, special types of screwdrivers for mobile phone repair are, STATE(-), PLUS(+), T4, T5, T6, T8, 3 STAR, 4 STAR etc. Always use the screwdriver by looking at the screw or both the screw and the screwdriver may be damaged.

CTC .

It is a liquid that helps to clean the board especially when a part is water damaged, board is washed with CTC, it solves 40% of problems without repair but Always protect the mobile's LCD from this, otherwise the LCD gets blurry or Spotfull.
